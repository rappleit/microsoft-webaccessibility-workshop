About Neko Atsume Cats

In Neko Atsume, players lure cats to visit their Yard with Goodies and Food. The player needs to exit the game in order for the cats to appear. Upon returning to the game, the player may see cats interacting with the goodies. There are many cats in the game, and each cat has a different range of items with which it will interact with. Therefore, the player has to set out different Goodies in order to be visited by all the cats.
